\section{Threats to Validity}
\label{threats}

\subsection{Internal Validity}
\label{sec:threats-internal}

Internal validity threats may arise from prompt sensitivity, inconsistent human annotations, and variation in agent outputs.
The study should mitigate these threats through fixed prompts, documented annotation guidelines, repeated runs where appropriate, and adjudication of uncertain cases.

\subsection{External Validity}
\label{sec:threats-external}

External validity threats concern whether results generalize across domains, requirement styles, and project scales.
The evaluation should report dataset characteristics clearly and avoid claiming generality beyond the studied requirement documents.

\subsection{Construct Validity}
\label{sec:threats-construct}

Construct validity threats concern whether the metrics capture the quality of requirement structure modeling.
The study should justify the mapping between metrics and the intended properties of structured requirement representation, especially completeness, consistency, and graph quality.
