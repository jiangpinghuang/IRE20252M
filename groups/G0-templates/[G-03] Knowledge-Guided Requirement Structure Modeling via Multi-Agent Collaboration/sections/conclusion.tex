\section{Conclusion}
\label{conclusion}

This paper studies knowledge-guided requirement structure modeling for intelligent requirements engineering.
The proposed paper structure centers on a focused research problem: transforming loose natural-language requirements into a structured, analyzable, and extensible requirement graph.
The methodology combines multi-agent requirement parsing, semantic decomposition, knowledge-guided modeling, graph construction, and human-in-the-loop refinement.
The experimental design evaluates whether this workflow improves structured requirement representation in terms of completeness, semantic consistency, graph quality, and representation accuracy.
