\section{Conclusion}
\label{conclusion}

This paper studies environment-aware requirement elicitation as a research problem in intelligent requirements engineering.
The proposed structure centers on multi-agent context understanding, knowledge-assisted requirement analysis, dynamic environment clarification, and human-in-the-loop validation.
The final paper should conclude by summarizing how the workflow improves requirement context completeness and by outlining focused future work on environment-aware elicitation only.
