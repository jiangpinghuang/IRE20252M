\section{Threats to Validity}
\label{threats}

This section follows standard empirical software engineering reporting practice.

\subsection{Internal Validity}
\label{threats:internal}

Potential threats include prompt sensitivity, evaluator bias, inconsistent stakeholder responses, and variability in agent outputs.
Mitigations should include fixed protocols, repeated runs where appropriate, and independent annotation or review.

\subsection{External Validity}
\label{threats:external}

The findings may depend on the selected requirement scenarios, domains, participant profiles, and environment categories.
The paper should state the intended scope of generalization and avoid claims beyond environment-aware elicitation.

\subsection{Construct Validity}
\label{threats:construct}

Metrics such as context completeness and clarification effectiveness may not fully capture elicitation quality.
The evaluation should define each metric clearly and align annotation rubrics with the research questions.
