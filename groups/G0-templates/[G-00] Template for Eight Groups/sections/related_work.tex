\section{Related Work}
\label{related_work}
In this section, we review the existing literature on multi-hop question answering (MHQA) and related areas. We categorize the related work into three main groups: (1) traditional MHQA approaches, (2) neural network-based methods, and (3) transformer-based models. For each category, we discuss the key contributions, strengths, and limitations of the approaches. We also highlight how our proposed method differs from and improves upon these existing works. Finally, we identify the gaps in the current research that our method aims to fill.
