\section{Experiments}
\label{experiments}
In this section, we present the experimental setup and results of our proposed method for multi-hop question answering (MHQA). We first describe the datasets used for evaluation, including their characteristics and the metrics employed to assess performance. We then compare our method against several baseline approaches, highlighting the improvements achieved. Finally, we conduct an ablation study to analyze the contribution of different components of our model and discuss the implications of our findings.
